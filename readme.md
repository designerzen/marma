Marmo Artist Platform
===


Analysing artist statistics from many sources has shown that there is an opportunity for established artists who are inactive to release new material. The resons for inactivity is usually distrust in the industry or a desire for self-publication. 

Marmo is a platform for established and emerging artists to establish funding for projects from fans.

By rewarding fans for participating in funding projects, the consumers get unrestricted access to both the final product and the process, with a direct to artist conversational messaging platform useful for engaging with fans and learning what the fans desire the most.

By rewarding fans for crowdfunding successful campaigns, a split from the distribution income is shared among funders, encouraging consumers to promote and publicise the material. 

Using a token system that fans are rewarded for funding artists, consumers are obligated to "donate" those tokens to emerging artists - giving us the data for which emerging artist to focus on and prioritise.

By receiving real income from the sales of the funded products, the consumer becomes part producer and feel a sense of ownership and pride in the material - a personalised and unique relationship is formed with the consumer and the artist, where through direct communication and requests, the musician is directed and guided by their fans. The success of the artist is shared among fans. 

Leveraging the reputation and fame of existing artists allows the elevation of emerging artists. The points earned by consumers for funding projects must be distributed to emerging artists. The more points these artists accumulate position them in more prominent positions on the website.

The website should be designed in monochromatic colours with sharp contrast, and splashes of our accent colour - cobalt blue. Typography should be easy to read, stylish and contain images that represent the topics. 

The website should show two artists - one established, and one emerging side by side. There is a donate button on both. The donate button will open a dialog that gives options as to how the user can donate real money and the perks that they receive will change based on how much money the user has donated.

Under both artists is qa top ten list that shows many emergin artists in a list with the amount of public tokens received next to each artist in the list. the artist with the most tokens is at the top of the list.
